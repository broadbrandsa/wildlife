Circular, icon-only control for toolbars, cards (save/share), media controls, and mobile nav. Always pass `label`.

```jsx
<IconButton label="Save sighting" variant="soft">
  <i className="ph ph-bookmark-simple" />
</IconButton>
```

Variants: `solid` (green), `soft` (sand fill — default), `outline`, `ghost`. Sizes `sm | md | lg`.
